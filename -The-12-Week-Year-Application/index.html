3 Sentence Summary

  1. The 12 week year breaks down the year into 4 “mini-years” with 12 weeks for work to provide urgency

    • The value of each moment is brought into sharp focus when there are only 12 weeks in your entire year

  2. Having a vision, measuring progress, accountability, and process control are integral to accomplishing the 12 week year

  3. Have a week in between each 12WY to reflect and plan the next one !
