The Future

Long-term goals are a double-edged sword because once they are met, velocity and direction waver.

One solution is to play the infinite game. This is a lesson from Simon Sinek’s book, The Infinite Game, which observes that naive business operate towards an end-goal, but the most successful operate through infinite values and limitless goals.
