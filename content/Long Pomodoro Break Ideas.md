# Long Pomodoro Break Ideas
## Principles
#### Mandatory
- 2x eating
- 1x walk/meditation
- 1x nap
#### Suggested
- 1x tidy up
- 1x escape simulator/game
- 1x anime
- 1x productive media
	- Chip away at productive playlist
- 1x texting/calling
