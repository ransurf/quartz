# Peg System
## Principles
- Remember numbers by combining letter sounds to form words
- To remember long sequences, pair them into words
- Visualize the craziness of the picture
## Peg Words
-   0 = s, x, z
-   1 = t,d
-   2 = n
-   3 = m
-   4 = r
-   5 = l
-   6 = sh, ch, j, soft g
-   7 = c, k, hard g, q
-   8 = f, v
-   9 = p, b
