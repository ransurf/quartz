# Properly Storing Clothes
## Hang
- Flowy fabrics
	- Complicated to fold
- Linen
	- Hanging causes creases
- Pants
	- Either long or folded
- Jackets
## Fold
- Thick hoodies
- Activewear
## Hang or Fold
- Shirts
	- Wider hangers prevent distortion
	- Modern materials reduce wrinkling
- Jeans
