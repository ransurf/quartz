# Open Statements or Predicates
`slides 7-8`
## Principles
- `x > 3`, `x has a brother`
	- Consists of 2 parts
		- First part `x` is variable/subject of sentence
		- Second part, predicate, property the subject can have
	- P(x) is a predicate with variable x
Types
- Unary means 1 variable, binary means 2, etc
