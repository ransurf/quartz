# Upper Body EZBar Workout
#### Timing
- 10 reps, don't stop for long
- 2-3 reps
#### Exercises
- curls
- tricep extensions
	- Like pulling your hoodie up
- overhead reps
	- pushing it to the sky
- upright rows
	- palms inward, lifting from knees to shoulders
- bent-over rows
	- the bayadas special
	- lean forward and lift
